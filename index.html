REHOBOTH FOUNDATION ORPHANAGE AND CARE HOME FOR THE ELDERLY

It's all about Care and giving Love

services

ORPHANAGE HOMES

We provide basic needs such as food, clothing, shelter, and medical care, as well as education and emotional support for the children under our care. We also offer counseling, therapy, and other specialized services to address the specific needs of children who have experienced trauma or other challenges. Help build a better future for vulnerable children in orphanages.

ELDERLY HOMES

We provide a safe and comfortable environment for seniors who require assistance with activities of daily living, such as bathing, dressing, and eating. In addition to medical care and assistance with daily tasks, many elderly homes offer social and recreational activities to help seniors stay active and engaged. At Rehoboth, we also cater to specific types of care, such as memory care for seniors with dementia or Alzheimer's disease, or skilled nursing care for seniors with more complex medical needs.

Specialized Care for people living with Disability

Specialized care for people living with disabilities aims to improve their quality of life, promote independence, and help them overcome the unique challenges they face on a daily basis. Physical therapy:- exercises and interventions. Occupational therapy:- ability to perform activities of daily living, such as dressing, grooming, and feeding themselves. Speech therapy:- To improve their ability to communicate effectively. Assistive technology: providing wheelchairs, prosthetics, and communication aids that help individuals with disabilities overcome barriers to mobility and communication. Personal care services: assistance with tasks such as bathing, dressing, and grooming, as well as other activities of daily living. Mental health services: specialized mental health services to address issues such as depression, anxiety, and other psychological challenges. Specialized medical care: Individuals with certain types of disabilities may require specialized medical care to manage and treat their condition.
